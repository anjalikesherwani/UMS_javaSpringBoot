LLB

The Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Legislative Law (LLB) is a three-year integrated law course that combines the study of arts subjects with law. The course helps students develop legal knowledge, analytical skills, and problem-solving abilities.
Career Opportunities: legal advisor, judge, assistant public prosecutor, and other roles in the government sector.

Course Name LLB
Course Duration 3-year
Admission Process Merit/Entrance
Eligibility 12th Pass
Average Annual Fees INR 50,000 to 70,000
