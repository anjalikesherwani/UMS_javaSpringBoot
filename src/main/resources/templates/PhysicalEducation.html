Master of Physical Education

The Master of Physical Education (M.P.Ed.) is a 1 to 2-year postgraduate program that deepens knowledge in sports science, advanced coaching, and physical education. It typically includes specialized subjects like sports management, exercise physiology, and sports psychology, along with research projects or practical training. Graduates can pursue advanced careers in teaching, sports administration, coaching, or academia.

Career Opportunities: Graduates with a Master of Physical Education (M.P.Ed.) can advance to roles such as sports coaches, sports managers, or physical education administrators. They may also find opportunities in sports research, fitness consulting, or academic positions at educational institutions. Additionally, they can specialize further in areas like sports psychology or sports science.

Course Name M.P.Ed
Course Duration 2-year
Admission Process Merit/Entrance
Eligibility Graduate
Average Annual Fees INR 3 lakh

Diploma in Physical Education

The Diploma in Physical Education (D.P.Ed.) is a 1 to 2-year program that covers fundamental aspects of physical education, including sports training, coaching, and fitness principles. It combines theoretical knowledge with practical experience in sports and physical activity. Graduates can pursue careers as sports coaches, fitness trainers, or physical education assistants.

Career Opportunities: Diploma in Physical Education can work as sports coaches, fitness instructors, or physical education teachers at various educational levels. Additionally, they can support athletic teams or community sports programs in training and development capacities.

Course Name D.P.Ed
Course Duration 2-year
Admission Process Merit/Entrance
Eligibility 12th passed
Average Annual Fees INR 30,000 to INR 70,000 per year.

Bachelor of Physical Education and Sports

Bachelor of Physical Education and Sports (B.P.E.S) is a 3-year undergraduate program focusing on physical education, sports science, and fitness training. It combines theoretical coursework with practical experience in sports coaching, fitness assessments, and health education.

Career Opportunities: A student of Physical Education and Sports Sciences can avail other career opportunities such as sports journalist, sports broadcaster, sports marketing manager, sports goods businessman, sports psychologist, sports physiotherapist, sports sociologist, sports biomechanics analyst, sports dietician.

Course Duration 3-year
