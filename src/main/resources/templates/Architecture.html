Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch)

Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch) is a professional degree focusing on design, planning, and construction of buildings. It prepares students for careers in architecture, urban planning, interior design, and sustainable development.
Career Opportunities: Roles in private firms, government projects, and independent practice as licensed architects.

Course Details B.Arch
Course Duration 5 years
Admission Process Merit/Entrance
Eligibility 12th Pass (45%)
Average Annual Fees INR 7,500 - 26.25 lakhs

Bachelor of Design (B.Des)

B.Des in Architecture is a 4-year undergraduate program blending architectural principles with creative design, focusing on innovative design processes and sustainable practices.
Career Opportunities: Architecture, interior design, and sustainable development, with potential roles in private firms, government projects, and independent practice.

Course Details B.Des
Course Duration 4 years

Master of Architecture (M.Arch)

A Master of Architecture (M.Arch) advances architectural knowledge through in-depth study of design, theory, and technical skills.
Career Opportunities: Architectural design, urban planning, and project management, leading to roles in design firms or government agencies.

Course Details M.Arch
Course Duration 2 years

Bachelor of Planning (B.Plan)

Career Opportunities: Roles in government, private firms, or non-profits focusing on transportation, land use, and sustainability.
