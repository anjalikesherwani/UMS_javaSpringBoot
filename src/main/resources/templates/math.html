Master of Technology

The M.Tech program generally lasts 2 years and involves advanced study in specialized areas of technology, often requiring a strong background in mathematics and engineering principles. Admission usually involves qualifying exams like GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering) and meeting specific eligibility criteria set by universities.

Course Name MTech
Course Duration 2-year
Admission Process Merit/Entrance
Eligibility 12th passed
Average Annual Fees INR 50,000 - 4 Lakhs

UG Mathematics Course: Eligibility

To be eligible to pursue undergraduate mathematics courses, candidates must have passed their 10+2 with at least 50% marks. In the case of merit-based admission, candidates must score at least 80-90% marks to be eligible for admission to a top college. For a BSc in mathematics, a minimum of 50% is required in mathematics or in aggregate in 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Students interested in BA in mathematics must pass 10+2 either in science or commerce with mathematics (as a compulsory subject) with at least 45% marks.

Course Name BA Mathematics & BSc Mathematics
Course Duration 3-year
Admission Process Merit/Entrance
Eligibility 12th Pass with mathematics
Average Annual Fees INR 3,000 to INR 1,00,000

Postgraduate Mathematics Courses

MA or MSc in Mathematics is a 2-year program that provides in-depth knowledge of Mathematics. The program includes advanced concepts like number theory, calculus, differential equations, etc. Admission to MSc in Mathematics usually requires qualifying entrance exams conducted by various universities/colleges.

Course Name MSc Mathematics & MA Mathematics
Course Duration 2-year
Admission Process Merit/Entrance
Eligibility Graduation in Mathematics
Average Annual Fees INR 30,000 - INR 4,00,000

Doctoral Mathematics Courses

A Ph.D. in Mathematics offers a wide range of opportunities to build a career. Students will enhance their research skills and deepen their knowledge in the field of mathematics. Typically, it takes 3-5 years to complete a Ph.D. in Mathematics. After completion, career options include roles in research, development, banking, business, statistics, and more.

Course Name Ph.D. in Mathematics
Course Duration 3-5 years
Admission Process UGC NET or CSIR UGC NET
Eligibility Postgraduate in Mathematics
Average Annual Fees INR 20,000 - 3,00,000
