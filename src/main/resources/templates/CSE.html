Computer Science and Engineering

Computer Science Courses After 12th are professional programs that provide opportunities for students interested in technology, programming, and problem-solving. The duration of these courses is usually three to four years. To be eligible, students must have completed the 12th standard with a minimum of 50% marks from the relevant board.

Advantages of Pursuing Computer Science Courses: Every program in the computer science field is specifically designed to train individuals with skills in high demand in today’s technologically oriented world.

Course Name Computer Science and Engineering
Course Duration 3-4 Years
Admission Process Merit/Entrance
Eligibility 12th Pass with 45% marks
Average Annual Fees ₹10,000 – ₹70,000

Bachelor of Engineering

A Bachelor of Engineering (BE) in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is a four-year undergraduate program that covers both theoretical and practical aspects of computer science, such as computer hardware and software, programming, algorithms, and data structures.

Main Subjects of Study: Data Structures, Algorithms, Operating Systems, Computer Networks, Database Systems.
Career Opportunities: Software Engineer, Network Engineer, Database Systems Designer, IT Manager.

Course Name Bachelor of Engineering
Course Duration 4 Years
Admission Process Merit/Entrance
Eligibility 12th Pass
Average Annual Fees ₹50,000 – ₹2,00,000

Bachelor in Computer Application

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is an undergraduate program that prepares students for careers in the IT industry. Subjects include Programming, Databases, Web Technologies, and Software Engineering.

Main Subjects of Study: Programming, Databases, Web Technologies, Computer Networks.
Career Opportunities: Programmer, Web Designer, Network Administrator, IT Support Staff.

Course Name Bachelor in Computer Application
Course Duration 3 Years
Admission Process Merit/Entrance
Eligibility 12th Pass
Average Annual Fees ₹30,000 – ₹1,50,000

Bachelor of Science

B.Sc Computer Science is a 3-year undergraduate program focusing on the technical aspects of computer systems. It prepares students for both higher studies and a wide range of IT roles.

Main Subjects of Study: Programming, Databases, Internet Technologies, Computer Networks, Software Engineering.
Career Opportunities: Software Developers, Web Designers, System Analysts, Network Administrators, Database Administrators, IT Project Managers.

Course Name Bachelor of Science
Course Duration 3 Years
Admission Process Merit/Entrance
Eligibility 12th Pass
Average Annual Fees ₹10,000 – ₹70,000
