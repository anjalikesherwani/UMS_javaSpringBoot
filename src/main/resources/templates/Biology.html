Bachelor of Science

B.Sc. in Biology is an undergraduate degree focused on the study of living organisms, covering topics like genetics, ecology, microbiology, and physiology, preparing students for careers in research, healthcare, and environmental science.

Career Opportunities: BSc graduates can pursue careers in Information Technology, Healthcare, Pharmaceuticals, Research, Agriculture, and more.

Course Name Bachelor of Science
Course Duration 3-year
Admission Process Merit/Entrance
Eligibility 12th Pass
Average Annual Fees ₹5,000 to ₹3,00,000 per year

Master of Science

M.Sc. in Biology is a postgraduate degree that delves deeper into advanced topics in biological sciences, including molecular biology, biotechnology, and ecology, equipping students for research, academic, or industry roles.

Career Opportunities: Career options include roles in IT, Healthcare, Pharmaceuticals, Research, and further doctoral studies.

Course Name Master of Science
Course Duration 2-year
Admission Process Merit/Entrance
Eligibility Graduation
Average Annual Fees ₹50,000 to ₹2,00,000

Bachelor of Technology in Biotechnology

B.Tech in Biotechnology combines engineering principles with biological sciences to develop technologies and products in fields like healthcare, agriculture, and environmental sustainability.

Career Opportunities: Diverse career options include roles in biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, and research.

Course Name BTech
Course Duration 4-year
Admission Process Merit/Entrance
Eligibility 12th Pass
Average Annual Fees ₹1.5 to ₹10.5 lakhs

Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery

Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS) focuses on Ayurveda, an ancient system of medicine from India, integrating traditional practices with modern medical knowledge.

Career Opportunities: Ayurvedic doctors, researchers, or roles in wellness centers and alternative medicine clinics.

Course Name BAMS
Course Duration 1.5-year
Admission Process Merit/Entrance
Eligibility One-year internship
Average Annual Fees ₹20,000 to ₹3,00,000
